## ESF Framework (Epistemic Stewardship Framework)
Required on every project:
1. **Design Intent** — creative spec written *before* AI touches code
2. **AI Direction Log** (3–5 entries) — what you asked, what AI produced, what you changed/kept/rejected and why
3. **Records of Resistance** (3 moments) — documented rejections/revisions of AI output
4. **Five Questions Reflection** — short paragraph before submission
   1. Can I defend this?
   2. Is this mine?
   3. Did I verify?
   4. Would I teach this?
   5. Is my documentation honest?
